The best movies according to Rodrigo

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Godfather

My favourite movie ever. I'm in love with mafia movies and I think this is the best one in this genre. Al Paccino's performance together with the story, the soundtrack and film's aesthetics make of this my favourite film.

The Shawshank Redemption

In my oppinion, one of the most beautiful films. The film mix happiness and sadness and show how hard life in prison can be.

Catch me if you can

One of the best DiCaprio perfomances. The funny way such an important story based on real life events is told seems perfect to me.
